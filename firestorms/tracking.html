Not much can be done to predict a firestorm ahead of time, but you can predict what wildfires may become massive firestorms depending on it's shape.

Wildfires that burn in expansive areas of active flames are more likely to form into a massive firestorm, while fires that burn in a narrow expanse most likely will not.

Wildfires can be ranked from 1-6 depending on it's visuals, and this would most likely be how firestorms are recorded.


Table of Fire Ranking
Fire Scale Fire Description
1 Smoldering Ground Fire
2 Low Vigour Surface Fire
3 Moderately Vigorous Surface Fire
4 Highly Vigorous Surface Fire with Torching, or Passive Crown Fire
5 Extremely Vigorous Surface Fire or Active Crown Fire
6 A Blow Up or Conflagration; Extreme and Aggressive Fire Behaviour.
