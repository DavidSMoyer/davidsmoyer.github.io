Firestorms can have extensive socioeconomic impact. It can devastate thousands of acres of forest, and damage any structures in it's way. Within a residential area, millions of dollars of damage can be done. Even after it's cleared the area, it is still not safe to return to. Physical, chemical, and airborne hazards may still exist in the area. These Firestorms can be deadly, able to kill up to 100 000 people!
