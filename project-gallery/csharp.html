C Sharp Projects

Graveyard Rush

Graveyard Rush is a first person shoot-em-up game. You spawn in a graveyard, with periodically spawning zombies. Use your pistol to kill them. Zombies can drop ammo boxes, med kits, grenades, and a golden gun powerup. Ammo boxes will replenish some of your ammunition, med kits will heal you, grenades can be thrown to instantly kill zombies in a radius but can damage you, and a golden gun will kill any zombie instantly when shot. After you eventually die, your score will be translated into points which can be used to buy custom particles, colours, and death effects.

Grappling

Grappling is a fun game where you make your way across a massive gap by grappling from object to object. You must make your way to the ending zone without falling or making contact with an obstacle.

Crystal Maze

Crystal Maze is a more horror-related project inspired by Dark Descent. The player spawns in the center of the maze with a monster. You must make your way through the maze and collect every gem. The monster will randomly wander said maze, looking for you. If you run and it is nearby it will follow your sound. It can see you from very far away if using your flashlight, but has a harder time and must be closer if you have it off.
