The Art of War

A unique strategy game. 2-4 players spawn opposite to each other, with a fortress and a customized hero. With 10 classes and 60 abilities to choose from, the customization element alone is fun. On the battlefield, construct buildings to obtain resources and train units. Use your hero and your army to overwhelm your enemy, and kill either their fortress or their hero.

Hallow's Fiend

An unfinished boss battle between you and a living magic pumpkin. Dodge it's attacks and attack it when it reveals it's weak spot to kill the pumpkin and save halloween.

Rock Paper Scissors

A simple game of Rock Paper Scissors. You can play against a friend or an AI. For player one, press Q, W, and E to respectively choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors. Player 2's keys are instead I, O, and P. The game is best out of 7. Whoever gets 4 points first wins.
